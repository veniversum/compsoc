Hi. I'm Qingzhuo

And I am running for President for CompSoc 2016/17.

Why I am running

Being in has been a truly amazing experience.
I care about this society deeply and want to bring it to the next level.
I want to be able to use my skills to provide you with an even better experience for members next year.

I will dedicate myself completely to the society and I am committed to bringing the activities that you as members want to see.

Together, let us foster an inclusive community for everyone interested in technology.

Here are my plans
for
in the next year

What makes CompSoc awesome are its members. That's all of you. ♥

My goal is to connect people who share a passion for computing, and spread the love for technology.

Spread the Passion

We have an awesome thing going on here and people are missing out on it.
Let's get more people (especially freshers and students outside informatics) involved in and make them feel welcomed!

Tech talks by the community and industry leaders on topics that you care about.

Workshops that let you explore new technologies (Rails, React, Express, etc) and empower you.

Restore weekly or fortnightly socials including pubs, movie/game nights, bowling, and more to keep you excited. Bring the community closer through fun and relaxed activities.

More events

Tech talks, workshops, and hackathons to provide you with many exciting learning opportunities.

Just want to chill? Look out for more frequent and regular socials for you to meet like-minded peers!

I believe that you can get more from being in CompSoc, and feedback is key to you getting what you expect.

The committee is here for you, and your satisfaction is our success.

Better Engagement

Have your voices heard - your feedback and opinion are important!
The committee will listen to you and make the way you want it.

My vision and plans for CompSoc to be a leading tech society does not end with my term.

I will strive to better prepare future committees for their term, and provide them with more resources and a stronger foundation than what we had. This includes detailed documentation and reviews for proceedings next year.

Pave the path forwards

Diversify sponsor pool, establish industry connections and strengthen relations with the School of Informatics.

This is just the beginning.
I will leave the next committee with the resources that they need to build on our success.
